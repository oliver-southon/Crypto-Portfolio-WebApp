Tutorial
Add Holdings

You can add holdings to your portfolio by pressing the large plus button.


Select A Coin

When selecting the coin name for your holding, use one that is automatically suggested in the dropdown.


Tip!

If you're buying the coin in real-time, or just want to use the current market price, you can tick the option like so.


Deleting a Holding

After adding your holdings, you can view real-time statistics on them. If you wish to remove one, press the trash icon on the left.


Two Options To Delete

If you have exited your position, you can enter the price you sold at. If you just wish to delete it permenantly, you can do that too!


Previous Trades

If you chose the option to 'sell' your holding, it will be added to your previous trades. Access it using the button at the bottom right.

Viewing Your Previous Trades

This will take you to your previous trades page. You can use this to track your cumulative gains/losses. This will remind you that you only lose money if you sell!
